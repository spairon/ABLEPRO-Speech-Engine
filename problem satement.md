Automated Speech Diagnostic Engine
Hackathon Project Proposal (Able Pro Solutions × BIG Foundation)
1. Problem Statement
First Round
Design and develop a working ML model, and demonstrate the following outcomes and
deliverables:
1. Code/Model that identifies, labels and displays the unique speakers from a given audio
sample(s).
2. Code/Model that generates transcriptions of each unique speaker from the sample.
(in Kannada Transcriptions) with their timestamp.
3. Identify and list out all the speech/acoustic parameters that your model uses to achieve
the listed classification requirements.
4. Use ML Algorithms and ensembling methods to Classify given speech samples as:
a. Adult (age > 18yrs) vs Child (age < 15 yrs)
b. Male vs Female
c. Typical vs Atypical

Rules of Engagement and points to remember
● The acoustic model is for the Kannada Language.
● You are free to use any library, data set, anything that you deem appropriate and useful
for the problem statement.
● A repository named speakathon has been created for this Hackathon.
● The repository will have an exclusive branch by your team-name, which would be your
primary storage for all work, source code, artefacts, documentations or deliverables.
● All of your work needs to be uploaded including readme.md, GetStarted.MD, source
files, instruction manual of your working model, configurations and any other material,
under your team’s respective branches in the same repository.
● Any identity information in the datasets need to be anonymized if not, otherwise.
● No personal identifiable data allowed in the submission, and you must customize labels
and information accordingly (e.g. speaker-1, speaker-2, male-speaker-1 etc.).
